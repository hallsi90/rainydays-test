Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 27.10.2024

This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined here.

Information We Collect

We collect information in order to serve you better. Here’s what we may collect:

  • Personal Information: When you create an account, place an order, or contact us, we may collect details like your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: We work with secure third-party providers to process your payments. We don’t store your payment card information on our servers.
  • Usage Data: To improve our site, we may collect data on how you interact with Rainydays, including pages viewed and clicks.
  • Cookies and Tracking: Cookies are small files we use to improve your experience. You can control cookie settings in your browser.

How We Use Your Information:

We only use your information for purposes related to our business. This includes:

  • Processing and delivering orders.
  • Providing customer support.
  • Improving our website and customer experience.
  • Sending updates and promotional offers (you can opt out anytime).

Sharing Your Information

Rainydays does not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Trusted Service Providers: For payment processing, shipping, and analytics, to help us run the business smoothly.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights of Rainydays and our users.

Security

We take data security seriously and work to protect your information. However, no online transmission is completely secure. By using our site, you acknowledge and accept these risks.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Update or correct your personal information by logging into your account.
  • Request that we delete your data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of marketing emails by following the “unsubscribe” link in any email we send.

Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party sites for your convenience. Rainydays is not responsible for the privacy practices of these other sites. Please review their privacy policies before using their services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. The latest version will always be posted on this page. We recommend checking it periodically to stay informed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us using our Contact Form.
